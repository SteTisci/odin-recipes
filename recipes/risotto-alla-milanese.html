Risotto alla Milanese

A classic Italian recipe from Milan

    Difficulty:

  • Average

    • Preparation:

  • 5 minutes

    • Cooking:

  • 25 minutes

    • Servings:

  • 4 people

Ingredients

  • Saffron pistils 1 tablespoon
  • Butter 125 g
  • Parmesan 80 g
  • Water to taste
  • Salt to taste
  • Carnaroli rice 320 g
  • Onion 1
  • White wine 40 g
  • Vegetable broth 1 liter

Preparation

    Step 1

  • Take the saffron and put it in a small glass, pour water on them just enough to cover the saffron completely, mix and leave to infuse overnight

    • Step 2

  • Prepare the vegetable broth, for the recipe you will need a liter

    • Step 3

  • Finely chop the onion so that it can melt during cooking and not be perceived while savoring the risotto

    • Step 4

  • Into a large saucepan, pour 50g of butter taken from the total dose required, melt it over low heat, then pour the chopped onion and let it stew for 10-15 minutes adding some broth so as not to dry out the saut√©ed

    • Step 5

  • Pour the rice and toast it for 3-4 minutes, so the grains will seal and keep cooking well. Deglaze with the white wine and let it evaporate completely

    • Step 6

  • Proceed with cooking for about 18-20 minutes, adding the broth a ladle at a time, as needed, as it is absorbed by the rice

    • Step 7

  • Five minutes before the end of cooking, pour the water with the saffron pistils that you had infused, mix to flavor and give the risotto a nice golden colour

    • Step 8

  • Once cooked, turn off the heat, salt, stir in the grated cheese and the remaining 75 g of buttermix and cover with the lid, let it rest for a couple of minutes, at this point the Risotto alla Milanese is ready
